Paper and Task form

Experimess is open to everyone. Please respect its purpose. To upload materials, you will need to register first. Register here. Don’t be shy! Upload your paper even if you don’t think it fits exactly in a category. Experimess is more about experimental tasks than games but we welcome all entries. Use the “tag” option to explain things about your entry that are not captured by the given categories. At this point, Experimess can only host links. To share experimental instructions, data, code, or other materials, please create an open, sharable link for them. Ready? Go!
